About Our Organization

Samantha Roberts

As the proud owner of Paws and Tales Shelter, I've dedicated my life to the welfare of our four-legged friends, providing them with love, care, and a second chance at life. Growing up surrounded by furry companions, my deep connection with animals led me to establish Paw Haven as a safe haven where dogs are treated with kindness and respect. My approach goes beyond basic needs; I believe in the power of love and companionship, ensuring each dog receives individual attention and training for adoption into loving homes. I'm also an advocate for animal rights, collaborating with communities, hosting adoption events, and educating the public about responsible pet ownership. Every dog deserves a loving home, and together, we can make a difference in their lives

Position: CEO and Owner

Ethan Joh

Together, Ethan and Samantha work tirelessly to provide love, care, and a second chance at life to dogs in need. Ethan is honored to work alongside her and contribute to making a difference in the lives of these deserving animals.

Position: Owner and Dog Care Specialist

Emily Dallas

Meet Emily, a passionate and dedicated dog trainer at Paws and Tales Shelter. Emily's love for dogs knows no bounds, and she has devoted her time and expertise to ensure that every dog in the shelter receives the best care and training possible. With years of experience and a deep understanding of canine behavior, Emily works tirelessly to create a positive environment where dogs can thrive and learn essential skills. Her patience, compassion, and commitment to their well-being make her an invaluable asset to the shelter and a beloved figure in the lives of the dogs she cares for

Position: Dog Care Specialist and Dog Rescue Coordinator
